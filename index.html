FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is the colour powder safe?
Yes. The powder is typically made from corn‑starch and food‑grade colour. If you have asthma or respiratory issues, you may want to wear a mask or bandana.
What should I wear?
A plain white T‑shirt shows the colours best. Wear clothes and shoes you don’t mind getting stained.
Will the colour stain my hair or skin?
It usually washes off with soap and water, but lighter hair may hold colour for a day or two. Oily hair products can help protect it.
Can I bring a camera or phone?
Yes, but protect it with a plastic bag or case. The powder can get everywhere!
